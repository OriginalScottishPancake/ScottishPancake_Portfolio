Vice Noire

Vice Noire was my very first passion project that I started in my first year of game development.

The game was based on a story I have written for an English Exam that same year, I did get a good mark for that story, it even got put in the 2024 SintLucas student story book that got sold on the christmas market!

Genre: Shooter

Platform: PC

Status: Finished Demo

Engine: Unity
